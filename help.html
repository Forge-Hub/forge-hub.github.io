What is cors?

Cors is a technique that allows a server to respond to a request from a different domain.

Consequences of allowing cors: Data breaches and unauthorized access from third-party websites.

By clicking the "Request Cors Demo" button, you will be granted a cors access for 2-3 minutes. After that you can use our algebra calculator without any errors.
